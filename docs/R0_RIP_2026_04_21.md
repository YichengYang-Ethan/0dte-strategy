## Honest Performance Bound

If A2 pilot had passed at 6.5% hit rate, expected net EV per trade:

```
EV = 0.065 × (+378% - cost_pct) + 0.935 × (-40% - cost_pct)
   = 0.065 × 3.49 + 0.935 × (-0.43)
   = 0.227 - 0.402
   = -0.175 per trade
```

**A2 pilot's 2× threshold was itself below breakeven EV.** Even
passing A2 would not have been a live-deployable strategy. The
mechanics of 0.20Δ OTM with −40% stop require WR ≥ 20% at these
payoffs, which corresponds to ~6× unconditional base rate, not 2×.

GPT Pro's 2× threshold was intentionally lenient to detect
"any signal at all." Fail at 2× means there is no prospective
signal, not even at the "much weaker than needed" level.
